\section*{Declaration on the Use of AI-based Tools}
This appendix documents the use of AI-based tools in the preparation of this thesis. A detailed tabular list of the tools used, as well as the purpose and scope of their use, can be found at the end of this appendix.

All suggestions generated by AI systems were independently verified, professionally evaluated, and revised or rejected by me as necessary. I assume full responsibility for the selection, interpretation, and final wording of the text.

This declaration follows the recommendations of the German Research Foundation (DFG).
